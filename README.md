AI Sector Trading Strategy: Finding Value in Market Gaps
This project builds a system to identify and trade pairs of AI stocks that have a strong historical connection. When one stock in a pair becomes too expensive or too cheap relative to the other, the system triggers a trade, betting that they will eventually sync back up.

What this project does:
Scans the AI Market: Analyzes 100 different AI companies to find the most reliable "partnerships" (like Dell’s hardware and Symbotic’s AI robotics).

Finds the "Sweet Spot": Tests different settings to find the best balance between making a profit and keeping risk low.

Real-World Testing: Validates the strategy on fresh market data (late 2025 – early 2026) to prove the results weren't just a one-time lucky streak.

High Performance: Achieved strong risk-adjusted returns, proving the strategy stays steady even when the market is volatile.

Key Results:
By focusing on companies with shared business goals—like AI infrastructure and specialized software—the strategy maintained consistent profits throughout the testing period.
